Mediconnect

Ideating, Prototyping, User testing

DATE: 2022-2023

ROLE: UX/UI designer, Product designer, Researcher

TOOLS: Drawing paper, Cardboard, People (interviewing)

OVERVIEW: One challenge that people face in the medical field is a lack of language resources. This project specifically targeted elderly non-English speakers. The goal of this project was to create a multimedium translation device and service to facilitate easier and more efficient communication between doctors, patients, and even third parties. To do this, we created an app that connects language-certified volunteers upon demand to patients and doctors. A physical board with an interactive remote/panel is used to aid in communication, as communication takes multiple forms.
