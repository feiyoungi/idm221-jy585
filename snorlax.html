The Snack Guardian, Snorlax

Coding, 3D printing, Building, Designing

DATE: 2022-2023

ROLE: Designer, Coder

TOOLS: Arduino set, 3D modeling program, 3D Printer, Paint

OVERVIEW: Arduinos are open source electronic programs that can do a multitude of things with code. In this project, my goal was to create an alarm that offsets when there is motion detected. This kind of device, dressed in a Snorlax (Pokemon) skin, will act as your dutiful snack protector. Should you need to leave your snacks alone, you can turn on the alarm to alert you at thievery attempts.
